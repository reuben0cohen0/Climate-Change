Your Role

So you may be wondering, what can you do to help combat climate change? You can:
Reduce your emissions by driving less - maybe take public transport as overall its less Co2 emissions per person
Reduce your electricity usage - often your electricity is made from fossil fuels
Reuse and recycle - the less you use or if you can use it multiple times, the less wasteage
Even just using your plastic waterbottle over and over again can save a little bit more rubbish form being burried in the earth for thousands of years.
